About Us

Universal Media Downloader is an open-source project designed to provide the cleanest, fastest, and most reliable way to extract media from the internet for personal archiving.

Technology

Built with FastAPI, yt-dlp, FFmpeg, and Vanilla ES2023, it ensures blazing fast downloads while maintaining an Apple-inspired Liquid Glass UI design.
